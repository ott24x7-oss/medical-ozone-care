About Medical Ozone Care

We help clinics, therapists and wellness practitioners across India adopt ozone therapy with confidence — through reliable, certified equipment and genuine, hands-on support.

Our Story

Precision ozone, made accessible

Medical Ozone Care was founded to bring dependable, clinic-grade ozone therapy equipment to practitioners who need accuracy and safety they can trust. We focus on a tightly curated range — the German-technology AOT-MD-520 generator, matching oxygen regulators, and genuine consumables — so every component works together perfectly.

From your first enquiry to long-term support, we're a direct line to people who understand the equipment and the therapy. We help you choose the right configuration for your cylinder and application, and we're here whenever you need us.
