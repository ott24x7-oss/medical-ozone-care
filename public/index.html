How It Works

From oxygen to therapy in four steps

Connect an oxygen source, meter the flow, select your level — clean ozone, ready to use.

Connect oxygen

Attach a medical oxygen cylinder or concentrator to the matching AOT oxygen regulator.

Set the flow

Use the regulator to set oxygen flow from 1/32 up to 1 L/min for your target dose.

Choose a level

Press M1/M2 and L1–L6 to dial in the exact ozone concentration you need.

Apply & destruct

Deliver ozone via the diffuser or luer; unused ozone routes safely to the destructor.
