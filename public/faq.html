Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our medical ozone equipment. Can't find what you need? Contact us.

What is the model number?
Our flagship medical ozone generator is the AOT-MD-520 (6-level, with built-in ozone destructor). We also offer a digital V/C-display generator and an ozone water & oil system. See the products page for the full range.
What is the ozone concentration range?
The AOT-MD-520 delivers 4–92.5 mg/L ozone, adjustable with oxygen across 6 calibrated levels (modes M1/M2, levels L1–L6). A printed concentration chart maps each oxygen-flow + level combination to an exact value — see the chart.
What accessories are included?
The AOT-MD-520 ships with a male luer lock (1), 3-valve lock (1), silicone hose (2 m), stone diffusers (2) and a 100–240 V power adaptor (1). Genuine replacement consumables are available in our accessory kit.
Is warranty available?
Yes — the medical ozone generators carry a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. See our Warranty Policy for details. Genuine spare parts and accessories are available.

Is it for professional use?
Yes. Medical ozone equipment is intended for use by, or under the supervision of, qualified professionals, in accordance with applicable local regulations. This website provides equipment information only and does not provide medical advice.
How is oxygen connected?
Connect a medical oxygen cylinder (or concentrator) to the matching AOT oxygen regulator, then run the silicone hose from the regulator to the generator's O₂ inlet. Set the oxygen flow on the regulator, then select the mode and level. See the setup guide.
